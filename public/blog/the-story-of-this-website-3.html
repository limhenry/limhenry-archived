3 months? So I have been missing for nearly 3 months. Whatever, so what actually happens during this 3 months? Nothing. I just doing what I suppose to do. I am a developer + student and blogger (sometimes, anyway). Anyway, I will talk about this in the next post.

Now, let's talk about what happen to this blog.

What's new

Initially, the URL for this blog is limhenry.me/blog and then I changed to blog.limhenry.me. But now I am going to change back to limhenry.me/blog, but don't ask me why, I have completely no idea...

Anyway, you might notice that the design of the website is totally redesigned. Why? The previous version of my blog was based (sort of) on Polymer 0.5, which is totally slow and unstable. By the way. Google introduces the new stable version of Polymer, Polymer 1.0 few weeks before Google I/O 2015. Since the new Polymer is come out, why I am not using it? I used Polymer last time just for Material Design, but Polymer have more than that. So, Material Design can be achieved by only using CSS. And the good news is, Google introduces Material Design Lite which allow us to add Material Design look and feel to the website just only CSS. Anyway, this Material Design Lite make my blog fast and nice.

Share

I added a few social features to this blog which are "Share to Facebook" and "+1 Button".

Now you can share the blog post DIRECTLY to Facebook in only 3 clicks. You can share by clicking the pink round FAB (Floating Action Button, this is what Google called it) and select "Share to Facebook". That's it. Simple right?

How about that "+1 Button"? So basically +1 is similar to Facebook's Like Button (Thumbs Up Button), just that +1 is from Google Plus, the social network owned by Google (but nobody is using it right?). The problem is, I still think Google Plus is nice, and that is the only reason I added that +1 button...

What happens to me

As I mentioned at the first paragraph, I have been missing from this blog for nearly 3 months. Actually I am busy with school stuff and development stuff. You can follow me on GitHub to see what am I doing, if you wish. Again, I will talk about that in the next post.

One Last Thing

Hari Raya is coming very soon and Selamat Hari Raya to all the Malay. Have a nice holiday. That's all from me. Bye bye.
